Movies Will Never Be the Same

Average movies entertain us. The best ones inspire us. So what can movies tell us about our own lives? Plenty! Grab some popcorn and find a seat—At the Movies premieres this July, only at Life.Church and Church Online for a limited time!

Grab a snack, find a seat, and let your imagination run wild – you’re At the Movies! Each week in July, you will catch a glimpse of popular movies and the message beyond their stories.

At Life.Church, you’ll be welcomed into a friendly, casual environment by people who are excited to see you – both at Church Online and in person. At each service, you can expect a thought-provoking and engaging video message based on the Bible, high-energy music, and more!
