Choose your favorite way to give.

You’ve got options when you’re deciding how to give at Life.Church: in person, online, on your phone, and more.

1

Campus Giving

While you’re attending experiences at Life.Church, you can use giving envelopes or giving drop boxes for your convenience and security.

You can make your gifts in person using cash, checks, or debit/credit cards.

You can always drop off or mail a check to your campus office.

Give at your campus

2

Smart Giving

Smart Giving is the easiest and best way to give at Life.Church. If you’d like to be a smart giver, you can give online or on your phone.

With the click of a button, you can:

  • Schedule gifts
  • Review your giving history
  • Update your contact information
  • And much more!

Start giving online

To use Smart Giving on your phone, just follow the simple steps below. Your giving will be applied to your debit or credit card, not your phone bill.

Tithing

Text GIVE and the amount of your gift to 86613.
For example, GIVE $250.

Offerings (learn more)

Text SPACES, RELIEF, or DIGITAL followed by the amount to 86613 to make your designated gift.
For example, SPACES $250.

Account Updates

Need to update your profile or account? Text EDIT to 86613.

3

Non-Cash Giving

Did you know you can donate non-cash gifts like stocks, bonds, mutual funds, or cars?

You can donate non-cash gifts like:

  • Marketable Securities: transfer ownership of your stocks, bonds, or mutual fund shares
  • Other Non-Cash Gifts: donate items like cars, boats, and jewelry
  • Life Insurance: name Life.Church as a beneficiary of your policy
  • Planned Gifts: give via retirement assets, bequests, trusts, etc. as part of your overall personal financial strategy.

If you are interested in any of these types of giving, please contact us at giving@life.church.

Ready to get started? Setting up a Smart Giving profile is a great place to begin.
